Let's a go! To celebrate Mar10 day, I made icons for the best plumber I know. I drew the icons in frames of 64x64 pixels with a 4 pixel stroke. I had a lot of fun making these, and I hope you like them! Next year I'll attempt pixel art icons with a 1 pixel stroke and a limited color palette.
